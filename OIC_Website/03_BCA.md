# Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA)

> *Affiliated Under Tribhuvan University (T.U) Kritipur*

![BCA at OIC](https://www.oic.edu.np/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/bca-college-in-kathmandu-1024x435.jpg)

## What is BCA?

BCA is a **four-year undergraduate degree** course in the field of computer applications or computer science. It is a technical degree that prepares students for a career in:
- Computer Applications
- Software Development

A degree in BCA is at par with a B.Tech/B.E degree in Computer Science or Information Technology. The basic course curriculum focuses on:
- Database Management Systems
- Operating Systems
- Software Engineering
- Computer Architecture
- Web Technology
- Programming Languages (C, C++, Java, Python, etc.)

## Mission of BCA Program

1. Understand fundamental concepts of Computers, Business environment and IT Applications
2. Successfully analyze technical data to reach actionable conclusions
3. Learn technologies & IT languages to address business problems
4. Develop competent technical writing skills
5. Identify and sharpen IT/programming skills

## Scope of BCA in Nepal

1. **Technical + Managerial Skills** - Provides necessary technical, scientific and basic managerial procedures
2. **Dual Competency** - Clarity on conceptual and application-oriented skills in commerce, Finance & Accounting
3. **Communication Skills** - Improved business management and tech support capabilities
4. **Higher Studies** - Option for MCA (Masters in Computer Application) or MBA
5. **Specializations** - Various focus areas available

## Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have:

1. ✅ Successfully completed **12-year schooling** (or equivalent) from any recognized University, Board or Institution
2. ✅ Secured a **minimum second division (45%)** or minimum C grade in all subjects

## Admission Process

1. Pass the entrance exam conducted by the university
2. Qualify the interview process
3. Selected candidates are eligible for admission
