# Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

> *Affiliated Under Tribhuvan University (T.U) Kritipur*

![BBS at OIC](https://www.oic.edu.np/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/bbs-at-orchid-college-kathmandu.jpg)

## What is BBS?

Bachelor of Business Studies is a **four-year program** conducted by the Faculty of Management of Tribhuvan University. The program focuses on business studies and trains students to develop intellectual and interpersonal skills.

BBS is a core management course that caters to the needs of the contemporary business world. The program covers:
- General Management
- Marketing Management
- Accounting

## Main Objective

To well equip students with leadership and managerial skills that help them compete in a global marketplace. Graduates become dynamic leaders and managers with the ability to handle responsibility in every sector.

## Scope of BBS

1. Develop students into competent managers
2. Prepare for constantly changing business environment
3. Broad understanding of organizational operation and management
4. Career opportunities in business, industry, and government
5. Entrepreneurship opportunities

## Mission of BBS Programme

1. Provide conceptual knowledge of business and administration
2. Develop attitudes, skills, and practical abilities for competent management
3. Encourage entrepreneurial skills for change in Nepalese society
4. Build foundation for higher management studies, teaching, research, and consulting

## Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must:

1. ✅ Have successfully completed **10+2 in business/commerce** or equivalent from recognized board/university
2. ✅ Satisfy any additional entry requirements prescribed by the Faculty Board

### General Requirements

- **Academic Year:** Minimum 150 teaching days
- **Instructional Hours:** 615 hours per year (150 days × 4:10 hours/day)
- **Daily Classes:** Minimum 5 periods per teaching day

## Attendance & Evaluation

- **Minimum Attendance:** 70% of classes held
- **Progression Rule:** Cannot appear in 2nd year exam without 1st year appearance

### Grading System

| Division | Percentage |
|----------|------------|
| Third Division | 35% |
| Second Division | 45% |
| First Division | 60% |
| Distinction | 75% |

## Graduation Requirements

1. Successful completion of 2000 marks with passing grades
2. Passing scores in all theory and practical components
3. Completion within TU's prescribed time limit
