# Bachelor of Business Management (BBM)

> *Affiliated Under Tribhuvan University (T.U) Kritipur*

## What is BBM?

Bachelor of Business Management (BBM) is a **four-year (eight semester)** degree course of Tribhuvan University. The main objective of this degree is to provide students with the skills necessary to perform **leadership roles** in the business and corporate world.

- **Duration:** 4 years (8 semesters)
- **Credit Hours:** 126
- **Focus:** Leadership and management skills development
- **Entrance:** CMAT examination required

## Mission of BBM Program

1. Develop creative thinking, decision-making, and leadership skills
2. Develop understanding of entrepreneurship and innovation
3. Prepare students to work as business managers and entrepreneurs
4. Create leaders who drive change
5. Build capacity for teamwork and organizational change
6. Develop critical thinking and problem-solving abilities
7. Foster understanding of ethics and social responsibility
8. Comprehensive understanding of all management aspects:
   - Operations
   - Human Resources
   - Marketing
   - Strategy
   - Accounting & Finance
9. Information Management in modern business settings
10. Broader understanding of Politics, Economics, Society, and Business Environment

## Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have:

1. ✅ Successfully completed **12-year schooling (+2)** from any recognized University, Board or Institution
2. ✅ Secured **minimum second division (45%)** or minimum Grade 1.8 in each subject
