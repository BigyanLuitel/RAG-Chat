## What is BSW?

Bachelor in Social Work (BSW) is an **undergraduate 4-year degree** that is awarded to individuals who complete their studies in the field of social work.

Orchid International College is one of the **top BSW colleges in Kathmandu, Nepal** with unique:
- Teaching Methodology
- Interactive Classrooms
- Regular Field Visits
- Camps and Social Activities

## About the Program

BSW is a professional approach to bring about change in social and human behavioral understanding. The curriculum includes courses on:
- Theory
- Research Policy
- Practice

This course enables social interaction and helps social work to flourish. It aims to produce **generalist social workers** to fulfill the needs of contemporary Nepal.

### What You'll Learn

- Apply theoretical knowledge in the real world
- Help masses suffering from:
  - Poverty
  - Violence
  - Depression
  - HIV/AIDS
  - Social/Gender Discrimination

## Field Work

Fieldwork is an **essential part** of the social work program.

### Requirements
- **Minimum:** 10 hours per week practical fieldwork
- **Evaluation:** Attendance, professional skills application, reflection

### Field Work Components
- Report Writing
- Individual Conference
- Group Conference
- Neighborhood Camp
- Rural Camp
- Urban Camp
- Block Placement
