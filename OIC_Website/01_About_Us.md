# About Orchid International College

> *Affiliated Under Tribhuvan University (T.U) Kritipur*

![About Orchid International College](https://www.oic.edu.np/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/where-is-orchid-international-college-930x582.jpg)

## Overview

Orchid International College affiliated to Tribhuvan University, was established in **2010 AD** which is promoted by a group of experienced and dedicated academicians, computer engineers, management professionals and social activists of Nepal.

It's a leading institution committed to providing exceptional education and nurturing versatile individuals in the field of:
- Information Technology
- Management
- Social Science

With integrated courses and well-rounded methodologies, OIC strives to develop students who are equipped to make a meaningful impact in the real world.

## Our Approach

Orchid International College understands the importance of practical application alongside theoretical knowledge. The dedicated team of educators ensures a meticulous blend of both approaches, enabling students to:

- Gain hands-on experience
- Develop deep understanding of subject matter
- Acquire skills necessary to thrive in real-time scenarios
- Contribute effectively to information technology, management and social sciences

## Faculty Excellence

OIC takes pride in its esteemed faculty, composed of the best teachers in various industries. Their expertise and guidance ensure that students receive a high-quality education, tailored to meet the demands of the ever-evolving professional landscape.

## Commitment to Accessibility

OIC is committed to providing **affordable education** to students. The college believes that access to quality education should not be a privilege but a right for all.

## Facilities

### Computer Lab
State-of-the-art computer lab for practice-based learning. Students can use computers at any time during college hours.

### Library
Excellent collections of books and journals on:
- Computer Science
- Management
- Social Science
- Mathematics
- Related subjects

The library also maintains collections of audiovisual resources, eBooks, dissertations, annual reports, and case studies.

### Digital Electronic Lab
Separate digital logic lab for rigorous experiments to consolidate basic knowledge in digital electronics.

### Organization Tie-ups
Partnerships with:
- Software companies
- Banks
- IT consultancies
- Derivative companies
- Manufacturing industries

These provide platforms for internships and job placement opportunities.
