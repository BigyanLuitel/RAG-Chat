## What is BITM?

Bachelor of Information Technology Management (BITM) is a **4-year, 8-semester program** offering an integrated Management and IT curriculum affiliated to Tribhuvan University.

BITM is a blend of technology and management which includes:
- **60% IT Content:** C, C++, Data Structure, Software Engineering, Web Technology, Graphics, Java, Network & Multimedia
- **40% Management:** Management and Social Sciences courses

Orchid International College is one of the top BITM colleges in Kathmandu, offering this program at affordable prices.

## Mission of BITM Program

1. Develop socially responsive and IT-oriented professionals
2. Develop skills in software development and database management
3. Develop ability in object-oriented software design methods and data management systems
4. Prepare students for postgraduate studies in Information Management

## Scope of BITM in Nepal

1. **Dual Skills** - Software development, database management + information managerial skills
2. **Versatile Careers** - Data Analyst, Survey Manager, Software Engineers for business/finance sectors
3. **Employment Opportunities** - Business companies, banks, financial offices, etc.

## Admission Process

1. Students of any field scoring 45% can apply for BITM
2. Pass the **CMAT exam** conducted by TU
3. Shortlisted candidates are interviewed
4. Selected candidates are qualified for admission

## Examination & Evaluation System

- **System:** Semester-based (8 semesters total)
- **Internal Evaluation:** 40% weight
- **External Examination:** 60% weight

### Requirements to Appear in Semester Exam
- Must secure minimum Grade 'C' or GPA 2.0 in internal evaluation
- Must secure Grade 'C' or CGPA 2.0 to pass semester
